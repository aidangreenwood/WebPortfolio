Here are the ICE assignments done by me throughout the semester:

  • Week 1: First HTML

    This first assignment was simply get something on the page, in fact the goal was to make as hideous as possible. Those were the good days, when things were simple and easy.

  • Week 2: Rough draft for this

    This assignment was a simple thing that we were doing in class and just following along for. I didn't know at the time, that this would in fact be a project later down the road.

  • Week 5: Prophet Card

    The goal of this assignment was to create one card in the middle of the page, that would display the prophet of the church, and give some information on him. Sticking with the theme of this class, that everything works off of each other, the next assignment would be very similar.

  • Week 5: Apostle Spotlight

    This assignment was very similar to the last assignment, but instead of it just being the prophet, it was the first presidency and the quorum of the 12 as well.

  • Week 6: Favorite Devo Table

    This projects goal, was to make a table and form, the table to list some of my favorite devotionals, and the forum was to ask for an input of the reader, on what their favorite devotional is.

  • Week 7: Grid Flags

    This week we focused on using grid; we specifically looked at how it can be used in a variety of styling ways. For this assignment, the goal was to align all the grid segments in order to create the flag for that given area.

  • Week 7: Positioning with CSS

    This assignment was very similar to the last, but we just focused on making sure evreything was in the right spot, spread out through the page.

  • Week 8: Image Gallery

    This assignment focused on reducing the quality of logo pictures and linking them to higher quality pictures. All it really did was change all the photos provided into jpegs, as they are "less quality" then put those on the page in place of the png's.

  • Week 8: Signup Form

    This assignment was probably the trickiest so far, as it required us to utilize all of the prior knowledge we had gained, and put it all into one page. this used a vast variety of skills such as, grid positioning, creating of containers, and flex to align evreything.
